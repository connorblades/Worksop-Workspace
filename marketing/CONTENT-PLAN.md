# Posting tips

- **Best times for a Worksop audience:** FB 7:30am or 5:30pm; IG 8am or 7pm; Stories anytime, but 12pm and 9pm peak.
- **Hashtags on FB:** drop them in the first comment, not the caption — keep the post itself clean. Keep it to 3–4 max.
- **Location tag every post:** "Worksop, Nottinghamshire". Tag local pages where natural (Bassetlaw District Council, Worksop Guardian, neighbouring businesses).
- **Re-use IG posts as Stories** with a "tap for full post" sticker — free reach.
- **Reply within 4 hours** during the launch window (last week of May → first week of June). The algorithm is deciding whether to push you in that window — answer everyone.
