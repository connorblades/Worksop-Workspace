The Studio · coming in Phase 3

A clean room for podcasts, photo, video.

£40 an hour. Acoustic treatment, broadcast-quality microphones, controlled lighting. Free parking. Coming soon.

One clean room. Use it for whatever.

We call it the podcast studio because that's most of what people will use it for. In practice it's a treated, controllable room: acoustic panels, broadcast microphones, good lighting and a door that closes. So it works equally well for podcasts, recorded interviews, video calls that need to look serious, brand photography, headshots, product shots, a place to record a course module, or anything else that needs a calm, neutral space.

£40 per hour when it opens. Members and non-members both. Currently in build. Opening as part of Phase 3. Register your interest below and we'll let you know the moment bookings open.

Everything you need to record

Turn up and press record. Everything is set up and ready.

Professional microphones

Broadcast-quality condenser microphones for up to 4 guests. Clear, warm audio as standard.

Individual headphones

Monitoring headphones for every seat so you can hear yourself and your guests clearly during recording.

Acoustic treatment

Purpose-built room with sound panels. No echo, no room noise. Just clean audio every time.

Video ready (planned)

The room is being designed so you can add a camera for video podcast content alongside the audio. Final spec confirmed nearer opening.

Fast broadband

900 Mbps dedicated fibre throughout. Stream live, upload files, or run remote guest calls without interruption.

Refreshments included

Tea, coffee and water included with every studio booking. No extra charges for the basics.

Why come to Worksop?

The nearest podcast studios are in Sheffield and Doncaster. Both are 17 to 18 miles away. Both charge more. And both require you to pay for city-centre parking before you have even started recording.

Worksop Workspace is easy to reach from across Bassetlaw and South Yorkshire. Harworth is 8 minutes away. Tickhill is 10 minutes. Maltby is 12 minutes. Retford is 15 minutes. Parking is free for every session.
