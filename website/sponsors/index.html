Worksop Workspace

Our Founding Sponsors

Two local property businesses backing Worksop's first dedicated coworking space.

Building a workspace from the ground up takes capital, conviction and people who believe in the project before the doors open. We are lucky to have both of our founding sponsors on board: businesses with deep roots in the property market across South Yorkshire and beyond, who saw the gap in Worksop's professional workspace provision and chose to back the answer.

Founding Sponsor

Bullseye Properties Ltd

Bullseye Properties Ltd is a UK property investment business helping investors take confident action on opportunities across the residential and commercial market. Their team has built a reputation for clear thinking, careful underwriting, and showing investors the maths before the marketing.

Their support of Worksop Workspace reflects the same instinct that drives the rest of their business. Identify a real gap, back it properly, and get out of the way of the people doing the work. We are grateful to have them as a founding partner.

Founding Sponsor

South Yorkshire Property Buyers

South Yorkshire Property Buyers is a cash buying service for homeowners across Sheffield, Rotherham, Doncaster, Barnsley and the wider South Yorkshire area. No estate agents, no chains, no waiting. Their tagline puts it plainly: sell your house fast in South Yorkshire, no agents, no delays.

They work with homeowners facing time pressure for a thousand reasons: probate, divorce, repossession, relocation, or simply wanting certainty. That same eye for genuine local need brought them to Worksop Workspace. They saw a town with professionals who needed a proper place to work and decided to be part of the answer.
