Getting here from Tickhill.

By car: south on the A60. Around 15 minutes, free parking on arrival. Open directions from Tickhill →

By bus: Stagecoach East Midlands runs regular services between Tickhill and Worksop town centre. The workspace is a 10-minute walk from Worksop bus station, or there's a bus stop directly outside Carlton Road. Stagecoach · Traveline plan a route.

By train: Tickhill doesn't have a station; closest are Worksop (8 miles south) or Doncaster (7 miles north). If you're coming from further afield, Worksop is on the Sheffield.Lincoln line (Northern) and the Robin Hood Line (East Midlands Railway). National Rail Worksop info.
