A proper base
for freelancers in North Notts.

Take a client call without the dog barking. Hit a deadline without the kitchen distracting you. £30 a week, rolling. No contract.

A workspace built for solo work, not the corporate week.

Worksop Workspace is a coworking space on Carlton Road in Worksop. Used by freelancers, consultants, copywriters, designers, bookkeepers, coaches and solopreneurs across Bassetlaw and North Nottinghamshire. £12 a day or £30 a week, rolling. Free parking, 900 Mbps fibre, free coffee, a quiet professional floor, a meeting room for client work, a printer for the times paper still matters.

We're the first modern coworking space in Worksop. The closest alternatives are Sheffield (~18 miles) and Nottingham (~30 miles). Neither is a 20-minute drive.

FAQs

The questions freelancers ask.

Where can I work as a freelancer in Worksop?

Worksop Workspace on Carlton Road is the first modern coworking space in Worksop. £12 a day or £30 a week, no contract. Free parking, 900 Mbps fibre, free coffee, a quiet professional floor. Designed for self-employed people who need somewhere serious to work that isn't a kitchen table or a café.

Is coworking worth it for a solo freelancer?

It depends on your day. If you're losing hours at home to distractions, or buying £15 of coffee a day in a café, the maths swings quickly. £30/week (about 2.5 days of pass-through coffee) is the natural starting point.

Can I take client calls professionally?

Yes. The main floor has a relaxed working volume that's quieter than a café. For long or confidential calls, the meeting room is £20 per hour.

Can I meet a client face-to-face?

Yes. The meeting room is bookable by the hour. There's also a reception area for a quick coffee before going somewhere else.

Do I need to commit to a contract?

No. Hot Desk Weekly is rolling. Pay one week at a time, cancel any time. Useful for freelancers whose workload is uneven.

What if I'm between clients and don't need it that month?

Cancel via the app. Come back when you've got new work. The current week is the only commitment.

Is there a meeting room for client pitches?

Yes. £20 an hour, members and visitors. Big enough for 4 to 6 people. See /meeting-room.

Can I print contracts and proposals?

Yes. Printer and scanner on site. A4 and A3, colour where needed.
