Hot desks, pay as you go

A flexible desk for the day or the week.

Drop in any time during opening hours. £12 for a day pass. £30 for a week, rolling. Cancel any time. Free parking, 900 Mbps fibre, free coffee. No contract, no setup fee, no fuss.

A desk when you need one. Nothing more.

A hot desk is the simplest way to use Worksop Workspace. You pay for the time you need. A single day or a rolling week. And you take any free seat on the main floor. No assigned spot. No commitment. Come in when work needs the focus and a real desk; skip the days you don't.

Used by remote and hybrid workers, freelancers, tradespeople doing admin, parents on inset days, people whose home Wi-Fi has gone down, and anyone who's simply tired of buying flat whites to justify a café seat.

FAQs

Common questions.

What is a hot desk?

A hot desk is an unassigned seat on the main floor. You buy a day pass or a weekly subscription, walk in, and take any free seat. Unlike a dedicated desk (where the same seat is reserved for you), hot desk seats roll over to whoever's next in.

How much does a hot desk cost at Worksop Workspace?

Day Pass £12 (drop in any time). Hot Desk Weekly £30 (rolling. Cancel any time). No setup fee.

Do I need to book a specific desk?

No. Hot desks are first-come, first-served. Walk in during opening hours and take any free seat.

What are opening hours?

Phase 1 manned hours are 8am to 5pm, Monday to Friday. From Phase 2 onwards, members will be able to access the space outside those hours via the member app.

What's the difference between Day Pass and Weekly?

A Day Pass (£12) is one day. Hot Desk Weekly (£30) is unlimited access for a week, rolling, paid by direct debit. The weekly pays for itself after about 2.5 days a week of usage.

Is there parking?

Yes. Free on-site parking with no time limits during opening hours.

Can I take video calls from a hot desk?

Yes. The main floor has a relaxed working volume. For long, sensitive, or private calls, the meeting room books by the hour at £20.

Can I cancel the weekly anytime?

Yes. The Hot Desk Weekly is rolling. Cancel via the member app whenever you like. There's no notice period beyond the current week.
