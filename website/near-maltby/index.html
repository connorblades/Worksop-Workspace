Getting here from Maltby.

By car: B6463 east then A60 south. Around 18 minutes, free parking on arrival. Open directions from Maltby →

By bus: Stagecoach East Midlands runs services into Worksop bus station, a 10-minute walk away. Stagecoach, Traveline plan a route.

By train: Maltby doesn't have a station. Closest are Worksop (~8 miles) or Conisbrough. Worksop is on the Sheffield to Lincoln line (Northern) and the Robin Hood Line (East Midlands Railway). National Rail Worksop info.
