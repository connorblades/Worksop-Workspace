Private room hire · Worksop

A private room for
confidential 1-to-1s.

A door that closes. Soft furnishings, warm light, fast Wi-Fi, free parking outside. £20 an hour. Members and visitors.

When the conversation needs to stay in the room.

Worksop Workspace on Carlton Road has a private meeting room used for confidential 1-to-1 sessions. Therapy, counselling, mediation, financial advice, mortgage reviews, life and executive coaching, HR conversations, immigration consultations. Acoustically separated from the main floor, with a door that closes, comfortable seating, warm natural light and a calm welcoming entrance.

£20 per hour. Free parking immediately outside. A short walk from Worksop station and the bus station. Used by practitioners whose clients live in Worksop, Retford, Tickhill, Bawtry, Maltby, Dinnington, Harworth and Clowne. And who'd rather not ask them to drive to Sheffield or Nottingham.

FAQs

The questions practitioners ask.

Where can I hire a private room for a 1-to-1 session in Worksop?

Worksop Workspace on Carlton Road has a bookable private room. £20 per hour, members and non-members. Free parking, 5-minute walk from Worksop station, on a bus route. Acoustically separated from the open floor, with a door that closes and comfortable seating.

Is the room genuinely private for therapy or counselling?

Yes. Separate enclosed space, closed door, acoustically isolated from the main floor. We don't put staff or other members in earshot during a booked session.

Who uses this room?

Therapists, counsellors, mediators, IFAs, mortgage brokers, solicitors, life and executive coaches, HR consultants, occupational therapists, immigration advisors. Anyone whose work depends on a private setting.

How do I book?

Through our member app for ad-hoc hours. For recurring weekly slots, email hello@worksopworkspace.com and we'll hold a regular time block.

Is there parking for clients?

Yes. Free on-site parking right outside, so clients can come and go quickly without negotiating a public car park.

Can I bring a vulnerable client here?

Yes. The space is built to feel calm. Warm wood, natural light, no harsh strip lighting, no commercial bustle.

How much does it cost compared to Sheffield or Doncaster?

£20 per hour. Sheffield dedicated therapy rooms run £15.£25/hour for smaller rooms, more in city-centre suites. Doncaster broadly similar. For a North Notts practitioner, this is closer and roughly comparable.

Can I have a regular weekly slot at the same time?

Yes. Recurring bookings welcomed.

Is it suitable for a confidential mortgage or financial advice meeting?

Same room, same price. The room has a desk and chairs, fast Wi-Fi for screen-share, and the door closes.
