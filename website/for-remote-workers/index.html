People whose office is in Sheffield, Nottingham, Doncaster or Leeds (or no specific office at all) and who'd rather skip the commute most days.

£30 a week for unlimited access, rolling. About the cost of three days of train fares.

Getting here

By car, train, bus or on foot.

By car

30 Carlton Road, Worksop S80 1PH. Free on-site parking, no time limits. A60, A57, A1 and M1 J30 all feed into Worksop. Open in Google Maps →

By train

Worksop station is a short walk away . Sheffield.Lincoln line (Northern) plus the Robin Hood Line to Mansfield and Nottingham (East Midlands Railway). Sheffield is roughly 35.45 minutes by train. Live times.

FAQs

The questions hybrid workers ask.

Is there a coworking space for remote workers in Worksop?

Yes. Worksop Workspace on Carlton Road is the first modern coworking space in Worksop. £12 a day or £30 a week, no contract, with free parking and 900 Mbps fibre.

How long is the commute from Worksop to Sheffield?

By car, ~18 miles and 40.60 minutes each way in rush hour. By train (Sheffield.Lincoln line, Northern), about 35.45 minutes plus the walks. Hybrid workers use Worksop Workspace on their non-office days to claw back 2.3 hours.

Is it cheaper than commuting to Sheffield or Nottingham?

For most people, yes. £30/week with free parking versus daily train fares (£8.£12) plus station parking. The bigger saving is time.

Do hybrid workers use it on their non-office days?

Yes. This is the most common pattern. Two days in the office, three days here.

Can I do video calls all day?

Yes. 900 Mbps dedicated fibre, a quiet floor, a meeting room for private calls.

Is it suitable as a permanent base if I work remotely full-time?

Yes. Hot Desk Weekly (£30) suits most. Dedicated Desk (£50/week) launches in Phase 2. Private offices from £125/week if you want lockable space.

Can I get the train from Sheffield, Nottingham or Lincoln if I have to attend a one-off meeting in Worksop?

Yes. Worksop station is on the Sheffield.Lincoln line and the Robin Hood Line. The workspace is a short walk from the station.

What if my employer wants to pay for it?

Many remote workers expense the membership. We'll provide a proper VAT invoice and a reference letter for HR.
