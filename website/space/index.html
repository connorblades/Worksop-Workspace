The Space

Worksop's only dedicated coworking space. Every environment, one building.

One building. Every way of working.

The ground floor opens with a communal hot desk area and private lockable offices, Monday to Friday. Quiet when you need it. A bit of background energy when you would rather have it. A private space for calls, meetings, or focused work when it matters. It is the only dedicated workspace of its kind within ten miles of Worksop. Free parking is right outside.
