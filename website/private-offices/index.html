FAQs

Common questions.

Is this a serviced office or a private office?

Both. They're the same thing under two names. A private office at Worksop Workspace is a lockable room for one company, all-inclusive (rent, electricity, heating, water, 900 Mbps fibre, cleaning, parking, tea and coffee) and rolling weekly with no setup fee. Some listings and aggregators call this a "serviced office" because it's fully serviced. Same product, two terms, one weekly price from £125.

How big are the private offices?

Each office is approximately 12 to 15 square metres. Sized for 1 to 4 people comfortably. Specific room sizes vary; get in touch if you have a specific team size or layout in mind.

How much does a private office cost in Worksop?

From £125 per week, rolling. The exact rate depends on the office (room size, position in the building). All-inclusive. No separate bills.

What does "all-inclusive" actually mean?

Rent + electricity + heating + water + lighting + 900 Mbps fibre + cleaning + free parking + free tea, coffee and water are all included in the weekly rate. No business rates, no separate broadband contract, no utility surprises.

Is there a long contract?

No. Rolling weekly. The current week is the only commitment. Most members give a week or two's informal notice before leaving so we can plan, but it's not enforced beyond the current paid week.

Can I put my company name on the door?

Yes. Branding the door is welcomed for serviced offices. We just ask that it's tasteful and in line with the building (no neon, no large external signs).

Can I receive post at this address?

Yes, for active members. Standard delivery only. Not a registered office service. We hold post at reception and notify you via the member app when it arrives. See our terms of business for the full detail.

How many people can use one office?

Most offices fit 1 to 4 people comfortably. Larger groups will need either two adjacent offices or a discussion about layout.

Can I have meetings in my office?

Yes. Your office, your meetings. For larger groups or pitches to clients, the dedicated meeting room books by the hour at £20. More on the meeting room →
