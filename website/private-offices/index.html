Private offices · also called serviced offices

A private office. From £59 a week.

Five lockable serviced offices in Worksop town centre. 6 m² to 20 m². Pick the one that fits. All bills, fibre, parking, refreshments and coffee included. Rolling weekly. No long contract.

A proper office. Without the overhead.

Worksop Workspace has five private serviced offices on the ground floor, ranging from 6 m² (a single-person solo office) to 20 m² (a team of 3 to 4). Each one is lockable, with your own key. All-inclusive: heating, lighting, 900 Mbps fibre, refreshments, parking, cleaning. You move in, you start working. No utility setups, no separate broadband contracts, no business rates surprises.

From £59 a week for the smallest office, £125 a week for the largest, rolling weekly. No long lease. Stop any time. The lightest-weight way to get a proper business address that isn't your kitchen.

FAQs

Common questions.

Is this a serviced office or a private office?

Both. They're the same thing under two names. A private office at Worksop Workspace is a lockable room for one company, all-inclusive (rent, electricity, heating, water, 900 Mbps fibre, cleaning, parking, tea and coffee) and rolling weekly with no setup fee. Some listings and aggregators call this a "serviced office" because it's fully serviced. Same product, two terms, weekly prices from £59 for the smallest solo office to £125 for the largest team office.

How big are the private offices?

Five offices, four sizes. Office 1 is 6 m² (65 sqft, solo). Office 2 is 12 m² (129 sqft, 1 to 2 people). Offices 3 and 4 are 14 m² each (151 sqft, 1 to 2 people). Office 5 is 20 m² (215 sqft, 3 to 4 people). See the "Choose your office" section above for the layout and current availability.

How much does a private office cost in Worksop?

£59 per week for the 6 m² solo office, £89 for the 12 m² office, £99 for either of the 14 m² offices, £125 for the 20 m² team office. All rolling weekly, all-inclusive. That's £30 to £47 per square foot per year all-in, which sits clearly below the local serviced-office market (BizSpace Doncaster publishes £26/sqft; Sheffield city centre runs £40 to £60/sqft) on the larger units, with a small-office premium on the 6 m² solo room that's still cheaper in absolute terms than anything else in town.

What does "all-inclusive" actually mean?

Rent + electricity + heating + water + lighting + 900 Mbps fibre + cleaning + free parking + free tea, coffee and water are all included in the weekly rate. No business rates, no separate broadband contract, no utility surprises.

Is there a long contract?

No. Rolling weekly. The current week is the only commitment. Most members give a week or two's informal notice before leaving so we can plan, but it's not enforced beyond the current paid week.

Can I put my company name on the door?

Yes. Branding the door is welcomed for serviced offices. We just ask that it's tasteful and in line with the building (no neon, no large external signs).

Can I receive post at this address?

Yes, for active members. Standard delivery only. Not a registered office service. We hold post at reception and notify you via the member app when it arrives. See our terms of business for the full detail.

How many people can use one office?

Most offices fit 1 to 4 people comfortably. Larger groups will need either two adjacent offices or a discussion about layout.

Can I have meetings in my office?

Yes. Your office, your meetings. For larger groups or pitches to clients, the dedicated meeting room books by the hour at £20. More on the meeting room →
