What's in the room

Everything you need for a productive, professional meeting.

Whiteboard wall

Large whiteboard with markers for workshops, planning sessions and brainstorming. Photograph and clear between uses.

Fast broadband

900 Mbps dedicated fibre connection. Dedicated meeting room wi fi so you are not sharing with the rest of the building.

Seats up to 10

Boardroom layout for up to 10. Classroom or presentation style for slightly more. Get in touch if you have a larger group.

Refreshments included

Tea, coffee and water included with every booking. Catering for working lunches can be arranged on request.

Free parking

Free on site parking for all attendees. No time limits. No charges. No scramble for a city centre space before the meeting starts.

A door that closes

A private, professional room that takes the meeting seriously. No coffee shop noise, no kitchen table.
