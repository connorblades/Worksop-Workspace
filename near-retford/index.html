The professional workspace Retford has been missing

If you live in or around Retford and work from home, there has never been a proper local option for when you need a professional space. The nearest coworking spaces have always been in Nottingham, Sheffield, or Lincoln, none of which are close.

Worksop Workspace is 15 minutes from Retford town centre, just off the A620. A quiet, focused, fully equipped workspace with free parking and free coffee included. No membership needed to drop in. No long contract required to take a dedicated desk.

Getting here from Retford

Worksop Workspace is on Carlton Road, Worksop, S81. From Retford take the A620 west towards Worksop. The journey is around 15 minutes by car with free parking waiting for you on arrival.
