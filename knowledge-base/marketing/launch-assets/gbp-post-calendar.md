## Image preparation notes

- Geotag every photo with the Carlton Road coordinates (53.3038, -1.1237).
- Rename files before upload. Use `worksop-workspace-hot-desk.jpg` not `IMG_3942.jpg`.
- Use the brand colour palette as backdrop wherever possible. Oak surfaces and linen walls dominate.
- Avoid bright fluorescent lighting. Morning or late afternoon natural light only.
- No staged group photos. One person in frame, working, is the brand standard.

## Posting workflow

1. Draft the post in the GBP dashboard on the morning it is due.
2. Attach the geotagged image.
3. Set the CTA button and link destination from the table above.
4. Schedule or publish.
5. Watch the listing for comments or questions in the 24 hours after each post.
6. Log the post in a simple spreadsheet so you can refresh ideas after week 12.

After week 12, run a short retro. Which post types drove the most clicks and direction requests? Build week 13 onward from what worked.
