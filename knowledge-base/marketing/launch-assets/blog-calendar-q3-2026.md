# Blog Editorial Calendar. June to August 2026

**Worksop Workspace** · Marketing · *12 week content cadence*

Status: Draft v1 · Last updated: 17 May 2026

Two posts per week is ideal. One per week is acceptable. The schedule runs week commencing 1 June 2026 through week commencing 17 August 2026. Each entry covers slug, title, primary keyword, audience, hook, outline, CTA and link destinations.

Topics pulled from the keyword gaps in `seo-keyword-research.md` and the Phase 4/5 commitments not yet covered. None of these duplicate the 24 posts already live on `/blog`.

Standing rules:
- 800 to 1,400 words for a standard post. 1,500+ for a cornerstone.
- Lead with a one sentence direct answer to the implied query. Bold the lead.
- Three or more internal links per post (to product pages, audience pages, other blog posts).
- One CTA at the end pointing at the page that converts best for the topic.
- Brand voice plain English, no em or en dashes, no banned phrases.
- FAQPage schema where the post has a Q&A block.
