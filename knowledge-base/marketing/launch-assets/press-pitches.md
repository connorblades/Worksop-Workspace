**Worksop Workspace** · Launch-readiness asset

One personalised pitch per outlet. Each under 150 words. Lead with the local economy angle, not the business.

Pitch one outlet at a time, not a press blast. Wait for a reply or 7 days, whichever comes first, before pitching the next.

## Before sending any of these

- Verify each outlet's current contact address. News desk emails change. A bounced pitch is a wasted pitch.
- Personalise the salutation with a journalist's name where you have one. "Hi" is acceptable if you do not.
- Do not attach images or PDFs. Attachments trigger spam filters and most desks reject them on principle. Offer them in the body, send when asked.
- Send between Tuesday and Thursday, mid morning. Mondays are full, Fridays are quiet.
- Keep a simple log of which outlet was pitched and when. If you have not heard back in seven days, send a one line follow up: "Hi {{name}}, just bumping this in case it got buried. No pressure either way. Connor."

---

## What to do after the first reply

- **Yes, send more:** confirm a building visit time or a call slot in the next 24 hours. Send photos, the comparison piece, and a one page fact sheet on the business.
- **Yes, but later:** ask for a date that suits. Honour it. Move on.
- **No, not for us:** thank them. Ask if there is a different journalist or programme that might fit. Move on.
- **No reply after one follow up:** stop. Re pitch in three to six months with a fresh angle.
