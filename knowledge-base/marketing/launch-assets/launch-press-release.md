# Launch Press Release

**Worksop Workspace** · Phase 9 pre-launch asset

A pre-written press release ready to fire on launch morning. Target opening: **Monday 1 June 2026**.

Format: standard press release. Headline, dateline, lead paragraph (5 Ws), 2 to 3 supporting paragraphs, quote from Connor, boilerplate, contact details. Under 400 words.

Send to: every outlet pitched in `press-pitches.md` plus any local journalist who replied to the earlier round of pitches. Send the morning of launch day (Monday 1 June 2026), before 10am, in the body of an email. Do not attach as a Word document. Editors paste from email, not from attachments.

## Supporting paragraph 1. Why now

```
The space has been built for the people who already live and work
in Bassetlaw and the surrounding catchment. Bassetlaw has more
remote workers than the national average, and until now, no walk-in
coworking option in the district. The closest comparable spaces
have been a 30 to 40 minute drive to Sheffield, Doncaster or
Mansfield. Worksop Workspace fills that gap for remote workers,
freelancers, tradespeople on their admin day, parents on school-
holiday weeks, therapists and small business owners across the
15 mile catchment.
```

---

## How to send

1. Subject line: `Worksop Workspace opens 1 June. press release attached`. Use "press release in body" instead of "attached" if you are not attaching anything. Most outlets prefer paste over attach.
2. From: a real human name and email (`connor@worksopworkspace.com`), not a generic press inbox.
3. Body: paste the release in plain text. Do not paste from Microsoft Word (smart quotes break in some inboxes). Paste from a plain text editor.
4. CC any second journalist at the same outlet you have built a relationship with.
5. Do not BCC a long list of outlets. Send each one individually with a personalised first line.

## Pre-flight checklist on launch morning

Before sending the release:

- [ ] Phone is on, voicemail is clear, set to "Connor at Worksop Workspace".
- [ ] Email inbox is empty enough that a journalist reply will not get lost.
- [ ] Building is open, lights are on, kettle is on. If a journalist takes you up on a visit it could be the next hour.
- [ ] The website is live, the booking forms work, the prices match the release.
- [ ] Three good high res photos are in a Google Drive folder, link ready to paste.
- [ ] Founding sponsors (Bullseye Properties, South Yorkshire Property Buyers) have been told the release is going out. They may want to share or comment.

---

## What this is not

- A press release for paid distribution (PR Newswire, Business Wire). Local press do not use the wires. Send directly.
- An announcement for industry coworking press (Coworking Insights, Allwork.Space). Send those a separate, longer story two to three months in once the numbers are real.
- A guarantee of pickup. A press release that gets picked up by one local outlet is a success. A release picked up by three is a strong launch. Five would be a remarkable week.
