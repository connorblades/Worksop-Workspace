# House Rules

**Worksop Workspace** · Members · *Phase 1 reference*

Status: Draft v1 — needs Connor review · Last updated: 14 May 2026

---

Welcome. Worksop Workspace is a place to work — quiet when you need it, community when you want it. These rules exist to keep that promise for everyone. Read them once. We'll never lecture you.

---

## The big ones

**This is a workplace, not a networking event.**
People come here to execute on their work — not to pitch, sell, or prospect. If you use the space to solicit other members for business, you won't be welcomed back. Have a friendly chat at the coffee machine? Of course. Hand someone a business card unprompted? No.

**Respect the focus.**
The ground floor has quiet zones for deep work. If someone has their headphones on or their head down, that's the signal. Take phone calls outside the quiet zone — a private office, a meeting room (Phase 3), the kitchen area, or outside.

**Be sound.**
Treat the space the way you'd want a colleague to treat your own office. Tidy after yourself. Wash your cup. Don't hog a meeting room past your booking. Be the member you'd want sitting next to you.

---

## The space itself

**Quiet vs social zones.**
Two atmospheres in one building. Ground floor (Phase 1): focused, calm, headphones-on energy. Basement (Phase 2): coffee-shop atmosphere, music, conversation. Pick the zone that fits what you're doing on the day.

**Phone and video calls.**
Quick calls in the quiet zone are fine if they're a minute or two and you keep your voice down. Anything longer or louder — move to a private office, meeting room, kitchen area, or step outside.

**Music.**
Headphones only. Never speakers. Even at low volume.

---

## Refreshments

**Help yourself.**
Tea, coffee, water, included. No need to ask, no need to buy something to justify being here.

**Wash your cup.**
The kitchen runs on the next-member-pays-it-forward principle. Wash what you use. If the dishwasher's full, run it.

**Food.**
You can bring food in. Eat at your desk, in the kitchen, or in the social zone (Phase 2). Strong smells (curries, fish) — kitchen only. Be considerate of the people working around you.

---

## Guests and visitors

**Members bringing a guest in for the day.**
Welcome. Sign them in at reception or via the member app. A day-guest counts as a day-pass visitor — same rules apply.

**Client meetings.**
Use the meeting room (Phase 3) or a private office. The hot desk area isn't the place for a multi-person meeting.

**Children.**
Worksop Workspace is for adults working. Children aren't permitted as members or day visitors. If you need to bring a child in briefly (e.g. a quick pickup or drop-off context), have a word with reception in advance.

---

## Personal property

**Lockers are provided.**
Use them. Don't leave laptops, phones, or wallets unattended on the desk. We're a community, but security still matters.

**Lost and found.**
Anything left in the space goes into the lost & found at reception. Email us if you think you've left something — we'll happily check.

**No leaving permanent setups.**
Dedicated desks are yours every day; you can leave your monitor stand, plants, whatever you like at your desk. Hot desks reset every evening — anything left out gets moved to lost & found.

---

## Cancellation, attendance, and the gym model

**No commitments.**
Weekly passes are rolling — cancel any time via the member app. No notice period. No penalty. Come back whenever you're ready.

**No attendance tracking.**
We don't care if you show up every day or once a month. You pay for access. Use it however suits your week.

---

## What gets you asked to leave

We're easy-going, but there are a few things we won't tolerate:

- Using the space to pressure or solicit other members for business
- Aggressive or threatening behaviour toward anyone in the building
- Persistent disregard for the quiet zones
- Damage to the space or equipment
- Anything that creates an unsafe environment for other members or staff

If something goes wrong, we'll have a quiet word first. If it doesn't stop, the membership ends. No drama.

---

## Politics, religion, and other charged stuff

**The space is neutral.**
We don't take positions, we don't host politically affiliated events at this stage, and we don't want the space used as a platform for one. Worksop Workspace is for everyone in Worksop — that means it has to feel welcoming to everyone in Worksop.

---

## Tell us when something's wrong

**Use the app.**
Anything broken, anyone behaving badly, anything you wish was different — raise it via the member app or have a word with whoever's on the front desk. We're building this place around the people who use it. Tell us what's not working.

---

## A free day pass for a Google review

If the space works for you, leaving a Google review helps a lot. As a thank-you we'll give you a free day pass to use whenever. Community gesture, not a transaction — no pressure either way.
