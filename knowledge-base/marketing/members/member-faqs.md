## The basics

**When are you open?**
Phase 1: Manned 8am–5pm, Monday to Friday. Future phases: 24/7 access via QR code for active members.

**Where exactly?**
30 Carlton Road, Worksop, S80 1PH. Worksop town centre, free on-site parking.

**Is there parking?**
Yes — free, on-site, no time limit. Rare in Worksop town centre.

**Do I need to book ahead?**
For hot desks: no, just turn up. For dedicated desks: yours every day, no booking. For private offices: it's yours, no booking. For meeting / podcast rooms (Phase 3): yes, book via the app.

---

## Pricing and plans

**How much is it?**
See the live pricing on worksopworkspace.com/membership. Headline: Day Pass £12, Weekly Pass £30, Private Office from £59/week. Pay-as-you-go hourly options too.

**Is there a contract?**
No long contracts. Weekly passes are rolling — cancel any time. No notice period.

**Do you offer discounts for committing for longer?**
No. Pricing is fixed. The deal is the deal whether you stay a week or a year. We don't bribe people to commit.

**What about an intro offer?**
Launch intro offers will be available at the start. After that, the only ongoing offer is a free day pass for leaving a Google review.

**How do I pay?**
Stripe. Direct debit for weekly subscriptions. Card for day passes and hourly bookings.

## Access

**Can I bring a guest?**
Yes. Sign them in via the app or at reception. They count as a day-pass visitor for the day — same house rules apply.

**Can I bring my child?**
This is an adults-only working space. Children aren't permitted as members or visitors. If you need to do a brief pickup or drop-off, have a word with us in advance.

**Are pets allowed?**
Assistance dogs always welcome. Other pets — not in Phase 1.

**Can I receive mail or packages here?**
Day-pass visitors no. Members on rolling plans, yes for the duration of your membership — but get in touch first so we can flag delivery expectations.

**Can I come outside opening hours?**
In Phase 1, no — Phase 1 is manned-only, 8am–5pm Mon–Fri. 24/7 QR-code access is coming in a later phase, included with your active membership when it launches.

## The space

**Is it actually quiet?**
The ground floor in Phase 1 is a dedicated quiet zone. Everyone there is focused, and that shared intent creates a naturally calm atmosphere. The basement (Phase 2) is more relaxed with background music. Either way, nothing like a café on a Saturday morning.

**Can I take a call from my desk?**
A quick one, kept quiet, sure. Anything longer or louder — step into a private office, meeting room, or outside.

**Can I eat my lunch here?**
Yes. Eat at your desk, in the kitchen, or in the social zone (Phase 2). Strong smells (curries, fish) — kitchen only please.

**Can I leave my stuff overnight?**
Lockers, yes. Hot desk surfaces no — they reset every evening. Dedicated desks yes, leave your setup as it is.

---

## Membership management

**How do I cancel?**
Through the member app. Cancellation deactivates your account at the end of the current billing period. No notice. No penalty. Come back whenever.

**Can I pause my membership?**
Cancel and re-join — it's effectively the same thing. Same price waiting for you when you're ready.

**Can I switch plans?**
Yes — via the app, or have a word with us in person. Switches take effect at the end of your current weekly billing cycle.

**Can my company pay?**
Yes. Many of our members are on a company card or invoiced through their employer. Stripe handles that.

## Phase 2 and Phase 3

**When does Phase 2 open?**
Once the basement fit-out is complete — date TBC. We'll let waiting list members and existing members know first.

**Will Phase 2 cost extra?**
Existing memberships will get access to the new spaces at no additional cost. Phase 3 podcast and meeting rooms are hourly bookings on top.

**Will there be a podcast studio?**
Yes — coming in Phase 3. Bookable by the hour, members and visitors. Same for the meeting room.

## Wi-Fi, IT, and the app

**Is the Wi-Fi reliable?**
Yes — high-speed, designed for the load of a coworking floor. If it's ever slow, raise it via the app and we'll fix it.

**Do you have printers?**
Yes — a shared printer is available. Fair use, no charges for normal volumes.

**What if the app isn't working?**
Raise it via the in-app issue form, or speak to whoever's on the front desk. We'll sort it.

## Anything else

**Can I host an event in the space?**
Possibly, in Phase 2 / 3 once the space is set up for it. Have a chat with Connor — we're open to community-led events that fit the brand.

**Can I sponsor something?**
We're not running sponsorships in Phase 1 — the space stays as a single brand. Worth revisiting in later phases.

**Is there a community programme?**
Coming in future phases — co-working lunches, founder breakfasts, speaker nights, chess club. We'll publish dates as they're confirmed.

Email hello@worksopworkspace.co.uk or raise it via the app. We'll answer it and add it to this list.
