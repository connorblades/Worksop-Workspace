# Member Agreement

**Worksop Workspace** · Operations · Legal & Compliance · *Phase 1 reference*

Status: **Draft v1 — needs Connor review.** Last updated: 16 May 2026.

---

This is the agreement every member signs (digitally at sign-up, or on paper for walk-ins) before using Worksop Workspace. It sits underneath the Terms of Business and is the personal, named-individual contract. The Terms of Business set out the commercial rules; this agreement attaches them to a specific person.

---

## 1. The parties

**Worksop Workspace** (operator), trading from 30 Carlton Road, Worksop, S80 1PH. Contact: hello@worksopworkspace.co.uk.

**The Member** — the individual named on the sign-up form. A Member is always a named individual, even if their company is paying. The named individual carries the contract obligations.

---

## 2. What the Member is buying

The Member is buying access to Worksop Workspace under the plan they select at sign-up:

- **Day pass** — one day of access. £12.
- **Week pass (one-off)** — seven consecutive days of access. £50.
- **Weekly recurring** — rolling weekly access, cancellable any time. £30/wk.
- **Private office** — a named office, from from £79/wk on bespoke terms.

The current Terms of Business (held at worksopworkspace.co.uk/terms) set out what is included with each plan. The Member confirms they have read the Terms of Business and accept them by signing this agreement.

---

## 3. Member commitments

By signing, the Member commits to:

1. **Be 18 or over.** Worksop Workspace does not admit under-18s.
2. **Use the space lawfully.** No illegal activity, no harassment, no sustained nuisance to other members.
3. **Keep their access credentials private.** No sharing of door codes, app logins, or wifi passwords with non-members. Bringing a guest into the space for a meeting is fine; setting up a non-member to work alongside you on your account is not.
4. **Pay on time.** For recurring plans, payment runs every 7 days via Stripe. If a payment fails, access pauses until it's resolved.
5. **Respect the [Member Code of Conduct](member-code-of-conduct.md)** and [Acceptable Use Policy](acceptable-use-policy.md).
6. **Take reasonable care of the space.** See [Damage and Breakage Policy](damage-and-breakage-policy.md).

---

## 4. What Worksop Workspace commits to

1. **Keep the space open** during the hours stated on the website and any signage at the door.
2. **Provide working facilities** — desks, wifi, refreshments, meeting rooms (bookable), parking, climate control.
3. **Tell you in advance** about any planned closures, building works, or material changes to the space or pricing — at least 7 days' notice for recurring members.
4. **Handle your personal data** in line with the [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md) and [Data Protection and GDPR](data-protection-and-gdpr.md) policies.
5. **Operate CCTV** in line with the [CCTV Policy](cctv-policy.md), with signage at every recording zone.

---

## 5. Photography and media

The space is regularly photographed and filmed for marketing. By signing, the Member consents to incidental capture in wide shots, atmosphere shots, and crowd shots of the space.

For any **identifiable** photo where the Member is the subject (interview, testimonial, named feature) — Worksop Workspace will ask for separate written consent using the [Photo and Media Consent](photo-and-media-consent.md) form. The Member can refuse without affecting their membership.

---

## 6. Cancellation

- **Day pass / week pass (one-off):** no refunds once access is granted, except as set out in the [Refund and Cancellation Policy](refund-and-cancellation-policy.md).
- **Weekly recurring:** cancel any time via the member portal or by email. Access continues until the end of the current paid week. No notice period.
- **Private office:** as set out in the individual office agreement.

Worksop Workspace can also end this agreement on notice — see Terms of Business §[refer to TOB termination clause].

---

## 7. Liability

- Worksop Workspace is not responsible for items the Member brings into the space (laptops, bags, coats, valuables). Members are advised to keep valuables with them and use the lockers where provided. See [Damage and Breakage Policy](damage-and-breakage-policy.md) §3 for what is and isn't covered.
- Public liability insurance is in place for incidents caused by Worksop Workspace's act or omission. The certificate is on the wall and available on request.
- Nothing in this agreement excludes liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence, fraud, or anything else that can't lawfully be excluded.

---

## 8. Changes to this agreement

Worksop Workspace can update this agreement with 14 days' written notice (email is fine) to recurring members. For one-off passes, the agreement that applied on the day of purchase is the one that governs that visit.

---

## 9. Governing law

This agreement is governed by the law of England and Wales. Any disputes go to the courts of England and Wales.
