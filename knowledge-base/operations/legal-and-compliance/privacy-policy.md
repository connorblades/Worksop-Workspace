## 4. How long we keep it

| Data type | Retention period |
|---|---|
| Active membership records | While the membership is active + 6 years after cancellation (matches Limitation Act for contract claims) |
| Failed-payment / debt-management records | 6 years from last activity |
| First aid records | 3 years minimum (HSE); 6 years if any incident occurs; longer where a claim is in train |
| Accounting and tax records | 6 years from end of relevant tax year (HMRC) |
| CCTV footage | 30 days, then overwritten — see [`./cctv-policy.md`](./cctv-policy.md) |
| Email and message history | While membership is active + 2 years |
| Marketing consent records | Until withdrawn; record of consent kept 2 years after withdrawal |
| Job applications (when hiring) | 12 months from decision |
| Tour / enquiry data (no signup) | 12 months from last contact |

When the retention period ends, data is deleted or fully anonymised.

## 7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

- **Be informed** about how we use your data — this policy
- **Access** the data we hold about you (Subject Access Request) — free, within one month
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Erase** data in certain circumstances ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** processing in certain circumstances
- **Data portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interests, and to direct marketing absolutely
- **Not be subject** to a decision based solely on automated processing — we don't do this

To exercise any right, email hello@worksopworkspace.co.uk with "Data request" in the subject. We respond within one month. We may extend by up to two further months for complex requests and will tell you if we do.

If we refuse a request or take longer than a month, we'll explain why and tell you that you can complain to the ICO.

If you think we've handled your data badly:

2. If unresolved, complain to the **Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)** at <https://ico.org.uk/concerns/> or 0303 123 1113.

## 9. Security

We protect personal data through:

- Access controls — only those who need to use the data can access it
- Encryption — in transit (HTTPS) and at rest for the database
- Backups — held securely, encrypted, retention-limited
- Staff training — when we hire, before they touch personal data
- Vendor due diligence — we only use processors with appropriate security standards
- Incident response — we have a written [`./data-breach-response.md`](./data-breach-response.md) plan

No system is perfectly secure. If a breach affects you, we'll tell you within 72 hours of becoming aware where there's a risk to your rights — see the data breach response plan.

## 10. Children's data

Worksop Workspace is an adults-only space (18+). We don't intentionally collect data about children. If we become aware that we've collected data about a child by mistake, we will delete it.
