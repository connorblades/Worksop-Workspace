## What data we collect from website visitors

1. **Things you give us** when you submit a form (express interest, contact us, book a meeting room, sign up for membership): name, email, phone (if provided), the message you typed, and whatever else is on the form.
2. **Things our forms backend captures** when you submit: the date and time, your IP address, the page you submitted from, and the interest tags you selected.
3. **Cookies and analytics** — see the [Cookie Policy](cookie-policy.md).

We do **not** collect: payment card data on this site (Stripe handles that on their own servers; we never see your card number), special category data (health, religion, etc.), or anything else not on the form.

## Why we use your data and our lawful basis

| What we do | Why | Lawful basis (UK GDPR Art. 6) |
|---|---|---|
| Reply to your enquiry | Because you asked us to | Performance of a contract / steps before a contract |
| Send you updates about the launch and the space (if you've ticked the box) | Marketing | Consent (you can withdraw any time) |
| Process your booking or membership | To deliver what you've paid for | Performance of a contract |
| Record CCTV at the premises | Security and safety | Legitimate interest |
| Comply with our legal obligations (accounting, tax, fire safety) | Because we have to | Legal obligation |

We do not sell your data to anyone. We do not use it for profiling or automated decision-making.

- **Go High Level** — our forms backend, which processes the form submissions and sends us the resulting leads. They are an EU/US-based platform with appropriate safeguards.

- **Accountant** — for invoices and tax.
- **Authorities** — if we are legally required to share (court order, HMRC, police).

We will never sell your data, and we don't pass it to third parties for their own marketing.

## How long we keep it

- Enquiries that don't become customers: 24 months, then deleted.
- Members and active customers: for as long as you're a member, plus 6 years after your last interaction (HMRC tax record requirement).
- CCTV footage: 30 days, then automatically overwritten, unless we've preserved a specific clip for an incident.
- Accident book entries: 3 years (HSE recommendation).

## Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

- **Access** the data we hold on you (a Subject Access Request — see the [SAR Procedure](sar-procedure-subject-access-request.md))
- **Correction** of inaccurate data
- **Deletion** of your data, where there's no overriding reason for us to keep it
- **Restriction** of processing
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable form
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Withdraw consent** at any time for anything we're doing on a consent basis

To exercise any of these, email hello@worksopworkspace.co.uk with "Data request" in the subject. We respond within one calendar month.

If you're not happy with our response, you can complain to the **Information Commissioner's Office** at ico.org.uk or 0303 123 1113.
