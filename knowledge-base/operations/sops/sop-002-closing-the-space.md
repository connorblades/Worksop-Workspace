# SOP-002: Closing the Space

**Worksop Workspace** · Operations · *Phase 1 reference*

Status: Draft v1 — needs Connor review · Last updated: 14 May 2026

---

The procedure for closing Worksop Workspace at the end of each day. Quick-reference tick list at `../checklists/02-daily-closing.md`.

**Timing:** Start closing checks 30 minutes before the official closing time (4:30pm for a 5pm close in Phase 1).

---

## 1. Final member check

1. Open the member app and check who's currently checked in via QR.
2. Walk the floor 30 minutes before close and let any remaining members know what time we close.
3. Ten minutes before close, do a final walk to make sure everyone is wrapping up.
4. By closing time, confirm the QR check-in list is empty — meaning every member who scanned in has scanned out.
5. If someone hasn't scanned out, check the floor — they may have forgotten. Walk them to the QR reader on the way out.

**Why this matters:** the QR check-in log is the source of truth for occupancy in a fire or emergency. It must accurately reflect who's actually in the building.

---

## 2. Reset the workspace

6. Wipe down all hot desks with a clean cloth and surface spray.
7. Realign chairs to their default positions.
8. Push any moved tables back into place.
9. Tidy stray items (water bottles, headphones, pens) into the lost & found bin at reception.
10. Take dedicated-desk-area items only if a member has clearly forgotten them — otherwise leave their setup as-is.
11. Reset the boardroom/podcast room to its booking-ready state if used today.

---

## 3. Refreshments

12. Turn off the coffee machine following the shutdown sequence in the machine's manual.
13. Empty the grounds container and drip tray. Rinse.
14. Wipe the milk frother wand.
15. Wash any cups, plates, or cutlery left in the kitchen — or run the dishwasher if there's a full load.
16. Empty the kettle if water has been sat for the day.
17. Check the fridge — discard anything past its date (especially milk).
18. Wipe down the refreshment counter.

See `../training/coffee-and-refreshments.md` for the full refreshments shutdown.

---

## 4. Bins and waste

19. Empty all bins on the floor (or whichever day is the rota day).
20. Bag and take to the external bins (recycling and general waste separated correctly).
21. Replace bin liners.

---

## 5. Technology and equipment

22. Shut down any communal computers or printers.
23. Power off the boardroom/podcast room equipment (TVs, mics, lighting).
24. Leave the Wi-Fi router and core network kit powered on — these never get switched off.
25. Make sure no laptops, phones, or chargers have been left out unattended. Anything that looks forgotten goes into the lost & found at reception.

---

## 6. Environment

26. Set the heating to overnight mode (lower setpoint, or off depending on season).
27. Switch off all lights except any safety/emergency lighting that runs 24/7.
28. Close all windows.
29. Close internal doors as appropriate (especially the office room doors — for privacy and fire compartmentation).

---

## 7. Lock up and secure

30. Final walk through every room. Confirm:
    - No members in the space
    - No water taps left running
    - No appliances left on (other than the fridge and Wi-Fi)
    - All windows closed
31. Set the alarm.
32. Exit via the front door and lock it.
33. Test the front door is genuinely locked.

---

## Common issues

| Problem | First action |
|---|---|
| Member won't leave | Politely repeat closing time. Offer them a quiet location to finish a final call if needed. Note the incident in `../../members/feedback-log.md` (if/when created). |
| Alarm won't set | Check all internal sensors aren't being triggered (door ajar, motion). Re-set. If still failing, contact alarm provider. |
| Found valuable item | Lock in the reception drawer. Log it in lost & found. Email the member if you can identify them. |
| Key won't turn | Contact locksmith from Drive's `07-facilities/suppliers-and-contractors.md`. Do not leave the building unlocked overnight — wait until secure. |

---

## Estimated time

30 minutes from start of closing checks to building secure.
