# SOP-001: Opening the Space

**Worksop Workspace** · Operations · *Phase 1 reference*

Status: Draft v1 — needs Connor review · Last updated: 14 May 2026

---

The procedure for opening Worksop Workspace each morning. Follow this every weekday. Quick-reference tick list at `../checklists/01-daily-opening.md`.

**Timing:** Arrive on site 30 minutes before official opening time (7:30am for an 8am open in Phase 1).

---

## 1. Arrive and access the building

1. Park in the on-site car park.
2. Unlock the front door with the building key.
3. Disarm the alarm at the panel inside the entrance. The code is in 1Password — never write it down anywhere on site.
4. Turn on the main lights from the panel near the entrance.

---

## 2. Environmental checks

5. Check the temperature. If the heating has been off overnight, switch it on now so the space is warm when members arrive.
6. Check ventilation — open windows briefly if the air feels stale, then close them before members arrive (especially in winter).
7. Walk the full floor and visually check:
   - Are the desks tidy and clear?
   - Are the chairs aligned?
   - Are bins empty? (If not, take them out as part of opening.)
   - Are toilets clean?

---

## 3. Technology

8. Check the Wi-Fi router is on and broadcasting (visible LEDs). If not, follow the Wi-Fi troubleshooting in Drive's `08-technology-and-it/wifi-and-network.md`.
9. Open a laptop and confirm a member-facing Wi-Fi network is connecting at full speed.
10. Power up the printer if used.
11. Power up the boardroom/podcast studio TVs and equipment if any bookings are scheduled today (check the member app for the day's bookings).

---

## 4. Refreshments

12. Switch on the coffee machine. Allow it to warm up before the first member arrives.
13. Refill the bean hopper if low.
14. Check the milk in the fridge — if there's not enough for the morning, restock now. See `../training/coffee-and-refreshments.md` for the full refreshment routine.
15. Boil the kettle once to warm it. Check tea, sugar, and other refreshment supplies.
16. Wipe down the refreshment area.

---

## 5. Front-of-house

17. Make sure the reception area looks ready — welcome pack stack, business cards, signage, any leaflets.
18. Check the day's bookings on the member app (private office assignments, dedicated desk attendance, day passes booked, room bookings).
19. Open the front door — the space is now live.

---

## 6. Within the first hour of opening

20. Greet every member as they arrive. Use their name if you know it.
21. Help any new members or day-pass visitors with Wi-Fi and locker setup (see `sop-003-member-onboarding-tour.md` and the tour script in `../training/tour-script.md`).
22. Make a mental note of who's in and who's expected. Use the QR check-in log on the member app as the source of truth.

---

## Common issues

| Problem | First action |
|---|---|
| Alarm won't disarm | Stay calm, re-enter code. If still failing, contact alarm provider from Drive's `07-facilities/suppliers-and-contractors.md`. |
| No Wi-Fi | Power-cycle router. If still down, follow Drive's `08-technology-and-it/wifi-and-network.md`. |
| Coffee machine error | Refer to machine manual. Common fix: empty drip tray and grounds container. |
| Heating not working | Check thermostat and pilot light. If still cold, contact maintenance. |

---

## Estimated time

30 minutes from arrival to doors open. Allow 45 minutes on Mondays or after a deep-clean weekend.
