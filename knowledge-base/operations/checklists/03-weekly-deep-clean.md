# Weekly Deep Clean Checklist

**Worksop Workspace** · Operations · *Phase 1 reference*

Status: Draft v1 — needs Connor review · Last updated: 15 May 2026

---

Done once a week — Sunday afternoon is the default slot. Closes any gaps the daily clean misses. Takes ~90 minutes for one person, ~60 minutes for two.

A separate brief for the external cleaning contractor lives in Drive's `07-facilities/cleaning-contractor-brief.md` once populated; this checklist is the in-house list.

---

## Floors

- [ ] Move all desks and chairs aside, hoover under
- [ ] Hoover hot-desk area, meeting room, kitchen, hallway
- [ ] Mop hard floors with brand-neutral floor cleaner (no scented)
- [ ] Spot-clean any stubborn marks on the oak floor (oak-floor wood cleaner only)
- [ ] Replace welcome mat / front mat if stained

## Desks

- [ ] Clear every hot desk surface completely
- [ ] Wipe desk surfaces with the wood-safe cleaner (oak surfaces are sensitive to harsh chemicals)
- [ ] Wipe chair arms and backs with antibacterial wipes
- [ ] Check every chair for damage — log if any need replacing
- [ ] Realign all hot desks to the standard layout (see floorplan in Drive)

## Kitchen and refreshments

- [ ] Empty the coffee machine fully — drip tray, grounds container, water tank
- [ ] Descale the coffee machine if not done in the last 7 days (process in [`../training/coffee-and-refreshments.md`](../training/coffee-and-refreshments.md))
- [ ] Wipe down the inside of the fridge
- [ ] Discard anything in the fridge over 5 days old (members are responsible for their own food, but communal-area expired items go)
- [ ] Wipe kitchen counters with degreaser
- [ ] Refill all consumables — coffee beans, tea bags, sugar, milk (UHT backup), water filter cartridges
- [ ] Check expiry on tea and ground coffee

## Toilets

- [ ] Bleach toilet bowls and rims (gloves and ventilation)
- [ ] Scrub sinks with limescale remover if needed
- [ ] Wipe mirrors with glass cleaner
- [ ] Restock toilet roll, paper towels, hand soap, sanitary bin liners
- [ ] Sanitary bins emptied (or contractor scheduled to)
- [ ] Wipe down all surfaces, including the toilet flush handle and door handle
- [ ] Replace air freshener cartridge if low

## Meeting room

- [ ] Wipe the screen with proper screen cleaner (not glass cleaner)
- [ ] Check the HDMI cable and dongles — replace if frayed
- [ ] Wipe table with wood-safe cleaner
- [ ] Realign chairs to default layout (boardroom, 8 places)
- [ ] Restock notepads, pens, tissues, water glasses
- [ ] Check the water jug — clean and dry

## Lockers

- [ ] Open and check every unassigned locker — empty if anything inside
- [ ] Wipe inside of empty lockers
- [ ] Check keypad batteries — replace if low (log indicator)
- [ ] Confirm assignment register matches what's physically labelled (members may have moved)

## Reception and entrance

- [ ] Wipe reception desk
- [ ] Restock welcome packs (Drive's print template)
- [ ] Restock business cards
- [ ] Check the day's bookings printout / app dashboard works
- [ ] Wipe the front door glass and handles
- [ ] Check signage — A-frame outside, opening hours sticker on door, sign-in QR poster

## Windows and natural light

- [ ] Clean any reachable inside windows (oak-frame interior surrounds — soft cloth only)
- [ ] Note any external windows needing the window cleaner — log in Drive's maintenance log

## Plants

- [ ] Water all plants (don't drench)
- [ ] Remove any dead leaves
- [ ] Move plants slightly so they don't tan-line the surface beneath
- [ ] Replace any plant that's clearly dying — Sage is the brand colour; dead plants undo it

## Tech and electrics

- [ ] Power-cycle the router and modem (weekly housekeeping — quick reboot)
- [ ] Check all visible cables — replace any frayed
- [ ] Wipe down the printer and check toner / paper
- [ ] Test fire alarm panel from the panel (don't trigger evacuation — there's a test mode)
- [ ] Check emergency lighting (a brief test cycle — quarterly full test is on [`./05-quarterly-fire-safety-check.md`](./05-quarterly-fire-safety-check.md))

## Outside

- [ ] Sweep the car park entrance and front pavement
- [ ] Empty the front bin if any
- [ ] Check the A-frame is upright and clean
- [ ] Walk the car park and pick up any litter

## Restock

- [ ] First aid kit — replace anything used in the week
- [ ] Stationery cupboard — pens, paper, sticky notes
- [ ] Print room — paper, toner if low
- [ ] Cleaning cupboard — wipes, sprays, cloths, bin liners

---

## Anything broken or noted

Log anything broken or worth flagging in Drive's `07-facilities/maintenance-log.md`. Don't try to fix things outside your competence — log and let Connor decide.

---

If this list keeps catching you out or feels missing something, edit it. This is a living document.
