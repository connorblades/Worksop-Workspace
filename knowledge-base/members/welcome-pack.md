## Wi-Fi

Network name and connection details are on the welcome card on every desk and on the wall by the coffee machine. Wi-Fi covers the whole building including the social zone in the basement (Phase 2 onwards).

If anything's slow or not connecting, raise it via the member app or grab whoever's on the front desk — we'll fix it.

---

## The member app

Download it from the App Store or Google Play. Search "Worksop Workspace". The app is how you:

- Get in and out via your **QR code** (scan at the front door — automatic check-in and check-out)
- **Book** a hot desk, dedicated desk, meeting room (Phase 3), or podcast room (Phase 3)
- See **real-time availability** of desks and rooms
- **Raise an issue** — anything broken, anyone behaving badly, ideas you've got
- **Manage your membership** — change plan, pause, cancel

QR scanning is also how we know who's in the building for fire safety. **Always scan in when you arrive and out when you leave** — it takes two seconds.

## In an emergency

The fire alarm is loud and obvious. If it sounds, leave via the nearest fire exit — these are signposted with green running-man signage. **Do not go back for laptops, bags, or coats.** Assembly point information is on the door of every room.

For anything non-emergency that's not right with the space or your experience, raise it through the member app or speak to whoever's on the desk.
