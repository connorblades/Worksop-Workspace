What is in the room

Everything you need for a productive, professional meeting.

65" AV screen

Connect a laptop via HDMI or wireless screen mirroring. Present slides, review documents, or run demos with everyone in the room.

Video conferencing

Built-in camera and display for remote attendees. Works with Microsoft Teams, Zoom, Google Meet, and any browser-based tool.

Whiteboard

Large whiteboard with markers for workshops, planning sessions, and brainstorming. Photograph and clear between uses.

Fast broadband

Gigabit connection for lag-free video calls and presentations. Dedicated meeting room wi-fi so you are not sharing with the rest of the building.

Refreshments included

Tea, coffee and water included with every booking. Catering for working lunches can be arranged on request.

Free parking

Free on-site parking for all attendees. No time limits. No charges. No scramble for a city-centre space before the meeting starts.
